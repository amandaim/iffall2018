6. www.maggiejia.com This website is the portfolio website of a graduated WashU student. I think it exemplifies effective design because, among other things, the pages are very responsive to the user. 7. www.eyeondesign.aiga.org This webpage is an article from AIGA's Eye on Design website. This webpage exemplifies effective communication because, as you scroll through the webpage and read the article, the images on the left also change to correspond to the writing. With this layout, you can continually see images while you are reading, making the reading experience more visual and engaging then traditional news outlet web formats.
